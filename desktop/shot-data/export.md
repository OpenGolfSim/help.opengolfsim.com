## Exporting Your Data

You can export your shot data as a `.csv` file to open in your favorite data analysis tool. This allows deeper analysis, sharing with coaches, or archiving your performance.

To export:

1. Go to the **Shot Data** tab
2. Click **Export CSV**
3. Select what data to export
4. Choose a location on your PC to save the file
