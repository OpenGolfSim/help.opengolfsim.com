# Third-Party Connectors

The golf sim community has built a number of unofficial connectors that you can use to try and get the shots flowing to OpenGolfSim from your launch monitor.

1. TOC
{:toc}

---

## Uneekor Third-Party Connector Bridge

A third-party connector bridge to OpenGolfSim for Uneekor and Foresight devices. Created by @SimAdmin on Discord.

1. Download the connector bridge application.
2. Open your OpenGolfSim and set your **Launch Monitor** to **Developer API**.
3. Open and start the connector bridge.
