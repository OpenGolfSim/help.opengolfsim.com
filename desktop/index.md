OpenGolfSim is a full golf simulator platform that brings together simulation, control, video, and analytics into one unified experience. At the heart of it is OpenGolfSim Desktop. A desktop application, available on Windows and macOS which includes a free simulator and connects to a number of launch monitors. It can also integrate additional hardware like a control boxes, mobile devices, and web cameras for swing video replay. All built to level up your golf sim experience.

This documentation walks you through installation, setup, and troubleshooting to get everything working smoothly.
